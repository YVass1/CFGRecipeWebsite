Welcome

Welcome to The Spicy Archives, a collection of tangy and (sometimes) hot recipes, that are bursting full of flavours. To make the dishes as easy as possible to follow, all the recipes are curated especially with in mind the following aims:
1.Simple Instructions. No ambigous confusing instructions here. No more, you will know when you know how much to put in, Mum!

2.Simple Kitchen equipment. Because who has everything??

3.Few tricks and tips. To give the recipes an edge.

4.List of vital and optional ingrediants. Again, who has everything always??


We are warning you right now, we are not called The Spice Archives for no reason, the recipes will need you to improve your spice rack game.
